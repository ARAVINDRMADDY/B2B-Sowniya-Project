How accurately can you identify a company's technology stack?
We utilize technographic tracking that monitors publicly facing technologies (like CMS, ad tech, and cloud hosting IPs) combined with job posting data and financial reports to deduce backend systems (like ERP and Security) with 85%+ accuracy.
What is your data replacement policy for hard bounces?
We guarantee a deliverability rate of 95%. IT decision makers change roles frequently, so if your hard bounce rate exceeds 5% within 30 days of purchasing the list, we will replace the bounced records for free.
Can I target companies based on IT budget size?
Yes. Using algorithms that evaluate total revenue, employee headcount, industry benchmarks, and active job postings, we append an estimated IT budget tier to each account.
