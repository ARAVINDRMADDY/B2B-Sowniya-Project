Our Rigorous 7-Step Scrubbing Protocol

We don't scrape and dump. We curate. Here is how we ensure 95% deliverability.

1

Multi-Source Aggregation

We extract raw contact data from highly reputable sources: CME seminars, state medical boards, healthcare journals, and hospital directories.

2

NPI Verification

Our AI cross-references extracted names against the National Provider Identifier (NPI) registry to confirm active licensing and precise specialties.

3

SMTP Ping Testing

We perform zero-bounce SMTP handshakes on every single email address. Hard bounces and honeypots are immediately purged from the system.

Human Quality Assurance

Before packaging your list, our data analysts manually review anomalies. A final compliance check against DNC and CAN-SPAM lists is executed.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you provide NPI (National Provider Identifier) numbers?
Yes, our 'National Campaign' and 'Enterprise' tiers include verified NPI numbers for every applicable physician in the database, allowing you to cross-reference data against CMS registries.
What is your data replacement policy for hard bounces?
We guarantee a deliverability rate of 95%. If your hard bounce rate exceeds 5% within 30 days of purchasing the list, we will replace the bounced records with fresh contacts absolutely free of charge.
Is this list compliant with healthcare marketing regulations?
Absolutely. We strictly adhere to CAN-SPAM, CCPA, and GDPR regulations. Our data is aggregated strictly from B2B sources and public registries, ensuring your cold outreach remains compliant.
Can I integrate this list into Salesforce or HubSpot?
Yes. All lists are delivered in universally compatible .CSV and .XLSX formats. The column headers are pre-formatted to map seamlessly into Salesforce, HubSpot, Marketo, and standard CRM tools.
