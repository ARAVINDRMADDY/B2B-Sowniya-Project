Frequently Asked Questions

How often is the US business database updated?
We perform a comprehensive update of our entire database every 30 days. This includes removing dead emails, updating job titles for contacts who have moved companies, and adding newly registered businesses across all 50 states.
Is the data CCPA and CAN-SPAM compliant?
Yes, absolutely. We strictly aggregate B2B (business-to-business) contact information from public directories and opt-in networks. Our data compliance team actively ensures we meet the standards set by both the CAN-SPAM Act and the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA).
Can I target specific US zip codes?
Yes. While standard lists are sorted by state, our custom enterprise plans allow you to filter contacts by specific cities, counties, or exact zip code radius to support hyper-local outbound campaigns.
What format will I receive the list in?
All lists are delivered via a secure download link in standard .CSV and .XLSX formats. These are universally compatible and ready to be instantly imported into CRMs like Salesforce, HubSpot, Pipedrive, or cold email tools like Lemlist and Apollo.
Do you offer refunds for bounced emails?
We offer a 95% deliverability guarantee. If your bounce rate exceeds 5% on our provided list (using a reputable sending domain), we will immediately replace the bounced credits with fresh data free of charge.
