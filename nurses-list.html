Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about purchasing and utilizing our nurse mailing lists.

How often do you update the Nurses Email List?
We run a stringent 45-day rolling verification cycle on our entire database. Additionally, whenever a custom list is requested, our automated system performs a real-time SMTP ping test right before delivery to guarantee a bounce rate of less than 5%.
Can I segment the list by specific nursing specialties?
Absolutely. You can filter our database by numerous parameters including Registered Nurses (RN), Nurse Practitioners (NP), Certified Registered Nurse Anesthetists (CRNA), Pediatric Nurses, Oncology Nurses, Location (City, State, Zip), and Years of Experience.
Is your data compliant with CAN-SPAM and GDPR?
Yes. All data provided by B2BLeadNex is strictly compiled from opted-in B2B sources. We ensure total compliance with major anti-spam legislation including the CAN-SPAM Act, CCPA, and GDPR regulations for international contacts.
What data fields are included in the final file?
Our standard export includes First Name, Last Name, Job Title, Hospital/Clinic Name, Verified Business Email, Phone Number, Mailing Address, City, State, Zip Code, Country, and Company Website URL.
What format will the list be delivered in?
You will receive an easy-to-use .CSV and .XLS format which integrates seamlessly into popular CRM platforms like Salesforce, HubSpot, Zoho, and any standard email marketing software.
