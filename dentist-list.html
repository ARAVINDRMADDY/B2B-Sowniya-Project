Do you provide NPI (National Provider Identifier) numbers for dentists?
Yes, our 'National Campaign' and 'Enterprise' tiers include verified NPI numbers for every applicable dentist in the database, allowing you to cross-reference data against CMS registries.
What is your data replacement policy for hard bounces?
We guarantee a deliverability rate of 95%. If your hard bounce rate exceeds 5% within 30 days of purchasing the list, we will replace the bounced records with fresh contacts absolutely free of charge.
Is this list compliant with dental marketing regulations?
Absolutely. We strictly adhere to CAN-SPAM, CCPA, and GDPR regulations. Our data is aggregated strictly from B2B sources and public registries, ensuring your cold outreach remains compliant.
Can I integrate this list into Salesforce or HubSpot?
Yes. All lists are delivered in universally compatible .CSV and .XLSX formats. The column headers are pre-formatted to map seamlessly into Salesforce, HubSpot, Marketo, and standard CRM tools.
