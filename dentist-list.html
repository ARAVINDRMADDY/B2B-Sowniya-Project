Our Intelligent Sourcing Framework

We combine AI scraping with manual human verification to ensure every record is legally compliant and actively practicing.

1. Multi-Node Aggregation

Data is sourced from ADA registries, dental conference attendee lists, medical journals, state licensing boards, and opt-in surveys.

2. NPI & License Check

Our algorithm cross-references every name against the National Provider Identifier database to confirm they hold an active dental license.

3. Live SMTP Ping Test

We execute a zero-footprint server handshake on all emails. Hard bounces, catch-alls, and honeypots are permanently deleted.

4. Human QA & Scrubbing

Our compliance team performs a final manual audit, stripping out generic role-based emails (info@, admin@) to maximize your inbox rate.
